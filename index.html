About Us

EXPLORING BEYOND

Phoenix United was established in August 2020 by two students of Bsc Computer Science at Chandiragh University, Sritha Zith Dey Babu and Saurabh Verma, with an aim to achieve something higher and give a big opportunity to the Junior students at CU this team was created, after its first successful Project at the NASA international Space app challenge with just 5 members, Phoenix United is now a big team with 20+ Members with 2 faculty advisors and big support from Chandigarh University. We do lots of research and big Internal Projects such as NASA, ISRO, CANSAT, and many more.
